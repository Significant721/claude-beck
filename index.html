Common questions

Before you buy.

My dog has never worn a muzzle before — is this still for me?
Yes. The protocol starts at zero. Day 1 is "your dog has never seen this object." We build from there. Most buyers are starting from scratch.
My dog already hates muzzles. Is it too late?
Not at all. The "Already Gone Wrong" recovery track is built specifically for dogs with a bad muzzle history. Most courses ignore this scenario. We don't.
What kind of muzzle do I need?
The protocol works with any properly-fitted basket muzzle. The included Muzzle Selection Cheat Sheet walks you through exactly which type to buy for your dog's breed, snout shape, and use case.
Is this balanced training? Are you going to use punishment?
No. Muzzle conditioning specifically is built on classical conditioning and positive associations — it has to be, or it doesn't work. Beck's balanced training methodology applies elsewhere in her work.
How long does it actually take?
The full protocol is 21 days with 5-10 minutes of work per day. Most dogs are wearing the muzzle comfortably by Day 14. Some take longer — that's why the troubleshooting guide is included.
What if my dog is aggressive?
This protocol is built for exactly that situation. The whole point of muzzle conditioning is to safely manage dogs who present bite risk. If you're managing aggression, this is more important for you than for anyone else.
