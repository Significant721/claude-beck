Should your dog be wearing a muzzle?

A quick assessment from a certified balanced trainer who's spent years working with reactive, bitey, and complicated dogs.

Muzzles aren't punishment. They're a safety tool that lets your dog do more — vet visits, grooming, walks past triggers, time around new people — without anyone getting hurt.

But most owners don't know if their dog actually needs one, what kind, or how to introduce it without making things worse. This assessment gives you a straight answer.
