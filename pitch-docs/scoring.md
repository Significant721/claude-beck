# Quiz Scoring Reference

6 questions, max possible score: **49**

## How tiers map

| Tier | Score | Badge | Result framing | Product shown? |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **High Priority** | 25–49 | "High Priority" (red) | "Your dog should be muzzle-trained — and soon." | ✅ Yes |
| **Recommended** | 12–24 | "Recommended" (yellow) | "Muzzle training would significantly help your dog." | ✅ Yes |
| **Preventive** | 5–11 | "Preventive" (green) | "Your dog doesn't urgently need it — but it's still worth doing." | ✅ Yes |
| **Optional** | 0–4 | "Optional" (green) | "Your dog likely doesn't need muzzle training right now." | ❌ No — soft email opt-in only |

## Question-by-question scoring

### Q1. Has your dog ever bitten or attempted to bite a person?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| Never | 0 |
| Once, in a clearly stressful situation | 4 |
| A few times, in different situations | 8 |
| Yes — and it's a recurring concern | **12** (max) |

### Q2. Has your dog ever bitten or attempted to bite another animal?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| Never | 0 |
| Once or twice | 3 |
| Multiple times | 6 |
| Yes — it's a known issue I manage around | **9** (max) |

### Q3. How does your dog handle vet visits and grooming?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| Totally fine, no issues | 0 |
| Stressed but manageable | 3 |
| Has to be heavily restrained or sedated | 6 |
| Vet or groomer has refused them, or required a muzzle | **9** (max) |

### Q4. Does your dog react to people, dogs, or other triggers on walks?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| No, walks are calm | 0 |
| Mild reactions, easy to redirect | 2 |
| Strong reactions that are hard to manage | 6 |
| We avoid walks or only walk at unusual hours/places | **9** (max) |

### Q5. Has your dog had any prior experience with a muzzle?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| No prior experience with muzzles | 0 |
| Wore one briefly with no real reaction | 1 |
| Has had a bad experience that needs to be undone | **5** (max) |

### Q6. Why are you taking this assessment today?
| Answer | Points |
|---|---|
| Just curious / learning | 0 |
| I want to be proactive | 2 |
| Something specific happened recently | 4 |
| A vet, trainer, or behaviorist recommended it | **5** (max) |

## Quick reference: typical scoring scenarios

- **Casual browser, no real issues:** 0–4 → Optional (no product pushed)
- **Anxious vet visits, mild reactivity:** 5–11 → Preventive (soft pitch)
- **Reactive dog without bite history:** 12–24 → Recommended (full pitch)
- **Bite history + reactivity + vet trouble:** 25+ → High Priority (urgent pitch)

## Quick test scores (no need to take the quiz to verify)

- Lowest possible score: **0** (all "best case" answers) → Optional
- One bad answer (e.g. dog has bite history with people, everything else fine): **12** → just into Recommended tier
- Reactive dog who's never bitten: ~**18** → solid Recommended
- Worst-case dog: **49** → High Priority (this dog is a known biter, can't be vetted, reactive on walks, already has muzzle trauma)

## Changes from v1

- 10 questions → **6 questions** (~60 seconds vs ~2 minutes)
- Dropped: grooming as separate question (merged with vet), scavenging, emergency muzzling, vulnerable people in home
- Email gate **removed** — result shows immediately
- Numeric score **removed from user-facing result** — fractions like "27/49" read as middling even when classified as High Priority. Tier badge does all the work now.
- Email capture card **removed from result page** — single primary CTA (the product) for v1. Email capture can be added later as exit-intent or thank-you page logic without cluttering the decision moment.
